        • Advertising with Type

          Attached Files:
          •  Good Examples.zip (2.537 MB)

          DESIGN CHALLENGE


          Create a magazine advertisement that communicates a message to a target audience, and features typography that is central to the overall design.

          -----------------------------------

          The Art of Rhetoric: Persuasive Techniques in Advertising

          https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FeCz5fy02JE

          -------------------------------------------

          Your design must follow these rules:



          1. You may not copy anything from an existing advertisement. (10 points)


          2. You must create your own text from scratch. (10 points)


          3. Your text should feature an alteration or effect. (10 points)


          4. School appropriate. (10 points)


          5. Dimensions 11 inches wide by 17 inches tall. (10 points)

          = 50 points



          OBJECTIVES


          • Use Layer Masks
          • Use Adjustment Layers
          • Alter images through color and shape (warp tool, liquefy tool)
          • Communicate to a specific audience
            • Are you selling a product, communicating a message, or both?
            • What is your Product and who wants to buy it?
            • Does your font choice work with the message of the ad?


          Some examples of effects and alterations (There are more! Research online)


          • Metallic On a Wall Shiny
          • Glowing Warped Blurry
          • Fire In Perspective Gradient
          • 3D Smoke Hand Drawn


          SUGGESTIONS


          • Use your own photography. Often its easier and less stressful to take your own photos instead of searching online for a photo that works with your design. There also the issue of using copyrighted material, which is against the law.

          • Make an ad for a product you are passionate about, or that you own personally.

          • Learn and be influenced by things that you see in magazines and online.

          • Build on ideas and make them your own, but do not steal work that was done by someone else.





        • Advertising With Type - Part II "Message"

          Write 1 paragraph describing your thought process behind the messaging in your advertisement.


          Questions to answer your paragraph

          1. Who is the advertisement representing? (What company or organization is it for?)

          2. Who was your target audience?

          3. Did you use Pathos, Logos, or Ethos as a tool to create an effective message?

          4. What other factors or ideas impacted your final design?

          5. Does your font choice and color play a role in your messaging?


          Part II = 20 points
